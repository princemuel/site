Hey there! Looks like your connection dropped, or maybe the network just blinked. Either way, the page you requested didn't load.

The good news: **the site is cached and you're still browsing**. Most pages you've visited before should work just fine.

## What won't work offline

- Images on pages you haven't visited yet
- Dynamic content like comments

## What you can do

- **Check your connection.** Wi-Fi, mobile data, or ethernet. A quick toggle off and on often does it.
- **Refresh in a moment.** If it's a blip, a retry should sort it out.
- **Come back later.** If you're on a plane, a train, or somewhere signal-sparse, the content will be waiting.

Nothing on this end is broken. It's just quiet out there right now.
